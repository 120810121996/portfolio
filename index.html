Featured Projects

E-commerce Customer Behavior Analysis

Comprehensive analysis of e-commerce customer behavior using a dataset of 350 customers with 11 variables. Identified key insights for improving customer experience and marketing strategies through exploratory analysis and linear regression.

Key Findings: Gold customers and San Francisco residents are the biggest buyers, with men spending slightly more than women.

Human Action Recognition

Developed a model for recognizing human actions in videos by comparing 3D CNN and MobileNetV2 with LSTM architectures. The transfer learning approach achieved 95% accuracy, significantly outperforming the 3D CNN model (72%).

Technologies: Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Transfer Learning

Climate Change Impact on Agriculture

Analysis of 10,000 data points revealing crop yield impacts from CO₂ levels and extreme weather events. Random Forest model predicts 20% yield decline risk by 2030 in vulnerable regions.

Recommendations: Crop adaptation, optimized irrigation, and CO₂ reduction strategies.

Tomato Leaf Disease Detection

Automated system for detecting and classifying tomato leaf diseases using YOLOv8 model. Achieved 92% accuracy in identifying 10 distinct disease categories, enabling rapid diagnosis and targeted treatment for farmers.

Impact: Supports sustainable agricultural practices and minimizes crop losses.
